The NLP Engine

How our models were trained

MindWave's backend runs three independent classifiers, all trained on labeled mental health text data:

Model 1
Emotion Classifier

Detects: anxiety, depression, stress, hopeful, calm, anger, loneliness

Model 2
Sentiment Analyzer

Classifies text as positive, neutral, or negative

Model 3
Risk Level Predictor

Assesses distress level: low, medium, or high — triggers escalation if needed

Architecture: TF-IDF (5000 features, 1-3 ngrams) → Logistic Regression (balanced class weights) | Trained locally via model/train_model.py
