Our Mission

Making mental healthcare accessible through AI.

MindWave was built for IdeaJam 2026 to solve Problem Statement #16 — AI-Powered Personalized Mental Health Support and Early Detection Platform.

The Problem

Mental health issues affect 1 in 4 people globally. 75% go untreated. The average time between first symptoms and treatment is 10 years. In India, there are only 0.3 psychiatrists per 100,000 people. AI is uniquely positioned to bridge this gap — available 24/7, stigma-free, and scalable to millions.

NLP Model Details

All models are trained by running model/train_model.py. The training pipeline:

  1. Dataset: 73 labeled mental health text samples (emotion, sentiment, risk) — augmented to 219 via synonym replacement
  2. Preprocessing: Lowercasing, URL removal, special character stripping, whitespace normalization
  3. Vectorization: TF-IDF with 5000 max features, 1-3 word n-grams, sublinear TF scaling
  4. Classification: Logistic Regression with balanced class weights, max 500 iterations
  5. Output: 3 .pkl model files + label encoders saved with joblib

⚠ Ethics & Limitations

MindWave is an educational/research prototype built for IdeaJam 2026. It is not a medical device and should not be used as a substitute for professional mental health care. The NLP models are trained on a small synthetic dataset and may not generalize to all real-world cases. High-risk detections should always be followed up with a qualified mental health professional.
